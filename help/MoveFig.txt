MOVEFIG - Fighter Move
          Move fighters onto or off of a planet

          figmove [p/s] {fighter amount} {all}

          [p/s]   - P indicates to planet from sector
                    S indicates to sector from planet

          {amount}  Number of fighters to move. Default
                    is All available Fighters.

          {all}  Will move fighters off of all your planets in sector.  
                 Only works with figmove s.
