                     
   
                <<<< moveship >>>>
   
     Moves empty ships from one sector to another.
                     
     moveship [sector] {back} 
                       
     [sector] - target sector
     [back]   - will grab ships from target sector and bring
                them back to current sector   
                               
                Can use either planet or SXX port in        
                starting sector for fuel.
